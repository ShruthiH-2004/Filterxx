Overview

Introduction to Text Extraction from Natural Images

In today's rapidly evolving digital landscape, extracting textual content from images is a foundational task with wide-ranging implications across numerous industries. From document digitization and smart surveillance to language translation and assistive technologies, the ability to automatically detect and interpret text embedded in images has transformed how humans interact with the world.

Unlike standard printed documents, real-world images often include text superimposed on varied, cluttered, and textured backgrounds. These images might contain uneven lighting, occlusions, distortions, or overlapping graphic elements that interfere with clear character visibility. A particularly challenging scenario involves graphical embossing backgrounds, where the text appears engraved, raised, or blended into the background.

Importance of Persian Language

Persian is an Indo-European language, related to English and other European languages, with a simpler grammar that lacks gender and has fewer cases than Arabic. Persian is the official language of Iran, while its varieties Dari and Tajiki are official in Afghanistan and Tajikistan, respectively. The modern language is written in a modified Arabic script. Persian has a long history and has significantly influenced other languages and cultures, from the Mughal Empire to modern English, which has borrowed words like "caravan" and "paradise".
